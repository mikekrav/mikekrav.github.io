My Writing

I write about product, branding, design, and technology—along with the occasional deep dive into AI and community building.

For years, I told myself I'd start writing and sharing my thoughts with like-minded people. But somehow, that goal kept slipping off my New Year's resolutions list. In 2025, I finally made the commitment: at least one blog post per month on the topics I love.

While I don't have the biggest audience (yet), and some posts may disappear into the void of the internet, I've found real value in the process—thinking deeply, refining my ideas, and sharpening my writing skills along the way.

Check out my articles below, and if something resonates (or totally misses the mark), feel free to comment or applaud. I'm always up for a good discussion.
